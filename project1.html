ZENITH

Product Design - Ceiling Fan

Zenith is a fusion of minimalism and innovation, featuring an avant-garde design inspired by the delicate beauty of flowers. It evokes serenity and balance, transforming the ceiling fan experience by seamlessly blending form and function. The introduction of toroidal propellers sets Zenith apart as one of the first ceiling fans to incorporate this innovative feature.

SOFTWARE & TOOLS

AUTODESK Fusion 360, Keyshot Studio, Raise 3D & Prusa 3D Printer

RESULTS

I really enjoyed the 3D modeling process of this concept and learned a lot using different tools. The development of the prototype for this concept helped me better understand assemblies and finishes in preparation for the mass production of the product.

AWARDS

Sulion Digital S.A.

Zenith was recognized as one of the best projects in the 3rd Edition of the Ceiling Fans with Special Features Contest, in collaboration with the University of Design, Innovation and Technology (UDIT).
