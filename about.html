about.

I'm a Product Designer based in Madrid, Spain

My journey as a product designer has been shaped by diverse national and international experiences, particularly in Europe, through projects I've worked on both as a student and during my professional internships. These opportunities allowed me to collaborate with multicultural teams, gaining valuable insights into global design practices and perspectives.

experiences.

I seek inspiration through the global language of design.

Beyond my professional work, I am deeply passionate about all forms of design. I frequently attend exhibitions showcasing everything from industrial design innovations to conceptual art and technology. These experiences continuously inspire my creativity and keep me attuned to emerging trends in the industry. My approach to design is rooted in creating products that seamlessly balance functionality and aesthetics, delivering meaningful experiences to users.
