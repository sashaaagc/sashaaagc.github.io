RAISS

Aviation Interior Design

In my third year, during my internship studing at UDIT, Universidad de Diseño, Innovación y Tecnología, a unique opportunity came up: collaborating with Iberia on a real-world challenge to improve the onboard experience on long-haul flights. That’s how R.A.I.S.S. — Restroom Autonomous In-flight Sanitising System — came to life: a full redesign of the Airbus A350 lavatory, driven by a clear goal — to make it more hygienic, inclusive, and comfortable for everyone on board.

TEAM

Guillermo Fernandez, Mateo Magaz, Jaime Campos, Pablo Gil & Antonio A. Gonzalez

SOFTWARE & TOOLS

AUTODESK Fusion 360, AutoCAD, Rhinoceros, Arduino & Keyshot Studio

RESULTS

Working on the prototype for this project at the University FabLAB was an amazing experience. I got to use all sorts of tools, like the 3Exes CNC, orbital sander, drills, and more, which gave me a hands-on understanding of how a design comes to life. It was really cool seeing how everything fits together and learning more about the details of assembly and finishes. It was a great way to build up my woodworking skills.

AWARDS

Crystal Cabin Awards - 2025

RAISS It was nominated at the 2025 Crystal Cabin Awards ceremony, held as part of the Aircraft Interiors Expo (AIX) in April 2025 in Hamburg, Germany. This event is recognized as the world’s leading platform for innovation and aircraft interior design.
