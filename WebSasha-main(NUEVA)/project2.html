HORUS

Product Design - AUTOMATIC WATCH

This concept is a classic-style watch inspired by timeless elegance and the strength of the desert. Its automatic mechanical movement ensures precision, while the army-style bracelet adds durability. The bronze finish with rose gold hues reflects the warm tones of desert dunes, making it a refined yet bold piece for those who appreciate detail and design.

RESULTS

This project was a personal challenge to tackle 3D modeling of a piece with very small dimensions, learning to work precisely on the tiniest details. It was part of the 3D Modeling course in my third year of university, allowing me to consolidate my knowledge and skills in a practical context.
